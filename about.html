If you are here to learn about me, then you are in luck! This website serves to fulfill that exact purpose. From my history to my current projects and adventures, The Green Gallery stands to document my life. Let's start with my history:
My name is Ruari Green. I am a student studying computer science in Idaho and am in the process of getting my bachelors degree. I was born in Pennsylvania and raised in Massachusetts. My father is a Chemist who shadowed as a computer scientist at his job, and it was there that I first fell in love with machines. My family moved to California to get closer to family, and that only helped to get me closer to computers, as the state is a major tech center. From there we moved to Idaho where I attended Meridian Technical Charter High School and got my first taste of actual work. I worked at the Reuseum for my internship during high school and I helped maintain their website and create a database for their backend inventory.
Right now I am studying at the College of Western Idaho, and I am in the process of transferring to Boise State University. At CWI I have participated in their Speech and Debate Team where I have competed at both a state and national level. I am currently working on several projects with some of my peers which should release within the next couple of years.
